Welcome to Maa Bhabatarini Enterprise

17+ Years of Excellence in Civil Engineering

Established in 2008, Maa Bhabatarini Enterprise has been a trusted name in the construction industry, specializing in building planning, design, land surveying, and site supervision. Our expertise also includes Total Station (TS) surveys, TRD layout drawings, elevation treatment, AutoCAD drafting, fire safety planning, and interior design.

Our services are not limited to these areas—we handle a wide range of construction-related projects, ensuring high-quality results for every client. Whether it's large-scale structural projects or custom architectural solutions, we bring precision, innovation, and reliability to every task.

Over the years, we have successfully completed notable projects, including ATI Kolkata College (Survey & Drawing, 2020), Shreerampore Girls College (Supervision, 2023), TRD Layout Drawing (Ongoing), and Red Cross Child Hospital (Fire Plan, 2023). Our commitment to quality and client satisfaction drives us to deliver the best solutions in the industry.
